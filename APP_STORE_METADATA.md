## Description – English

Valutakalk makes currency conversion simple – fast, clean, and ad-free.

Enter an amount, select your currencies, and see results instantly. Perfect for travel, online shopping, or everyday currency checks.

FEATURES:
• Fast conversion between 150+ currencies
• Daily updated exchange rates
• Shopping cart – add multiple conversions and see the total
• Favorite currencies for quick access
• Quick amount buttons (10, 25, 50, 100, 500)
• Dark mode and light theme
• Norwegian and English language support
• Works offline with last cached rates

PRO – OFFLINE MODE (optional subscription):
• Full offline access to cached rates
• Unlimited favorites
• Historical rate trends and charts
• Advanced rate overview
• iOS home screen widget

Valutakalk does not collect personal information. No login required.

Rates are sourced from ExchangeRate-API and are indicative. Always verify with your bank or broker before final transactions.

Subscription:
• Pro: 9 NOK/month with 7-day free trial
